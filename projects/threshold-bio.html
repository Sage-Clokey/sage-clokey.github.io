  <!-- PROBLEM -->
  <section id="problem">
    <p class="section-label">The Problem</p>
    <h2>Nitrogen Fertilizer is Broken</h2>
    <p class="section-intro">
      Modern agriculture is addicted to synthetic nitrogen. It costs a trillion dollars a year, wastes half of what's applied, and is accelerating a global environmental crisis — yet staple crops cannot exist without it.
    </p>
    <div class="problem-grid">
      <div class="problem-card">
        <span class="stat">$100B+</span>
        <h3>Global Fertilizer Market</h3>
        <p>Synthetic nitrogen fertilizer underpins the food supply for ~4 billion people. The market is projected to grow at 2.5% CAGR through 2030 — with zero durable alternatives.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="problem-card">
        <span class="stat">&lt;50%</span>
        <h3>Nitrogen Use Efficiency</h3>
        <p>More than half of all applied fertilizer is lost — leached into groundwater, volatilized into N₂O, or washed into waterways. Farmers pay for inputs they never capture.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="problem-card">
        <span class="stat">1–2%</span>
        <h3>Global Energy Consumed</h3>
        <p>The Haber-Bosch process — the century-old method for making synthetic nitrogen — consumes 1-2% of total global energy output annually, producing 150M metric tons of fertilizer.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="problem-card">
        <span class="stat">265×</span>
        <h3>N₂O vs. CO₂ Warming</h3>
        <p>Nitrous oxide from over-fertilization has 265x the warming potential of CO₂ over 100 years. Agricultural nitrogen is responsible for ~10% of U.S. greenhouse gas emissions.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- SOLUTION -->
  <section id="solution">
    <p class="section-label">Our Solution</p>
    <h2>Nitrogen Fixation, Built Into the Seed</h2>
    <p class="section-intro">
      We integrate a synthetic, plant-optimized <em>nif</em> gene cassette directly into the crop genome — giving plants the ability to convert atmospheric nitrogen into ammonia inside their own chloroplasts.
    </p>
    <div class="solution-layout">
      <div class="solution-visual">
        <div class="dna-steps">
          <div class="dna-step">
            <div class="step-num">1</div>
            <div class="step-text">
              <h4>Synthetic nif Gene Cassette</h4>
              <p>Co-optimized expression of nifH, nifD, nifK, and 9 accessory genes from A. vinelandii — codon-optimized for monocot plant expression.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="dna-step">
            <div class="step-num">2</div>
            <div class="step-text">
              <h4>Chloroplast Targeting</h4>
              <p>Nitrogenase components are routed to the chloroplast stroma — a natural reducing environment with partial oxygen exclusion, Fe-S biosynthetic machinery, and abundant reductant.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="dna-step">
            <div class="step-num">3</div>
            <div class="step-text">
              <h4>Stable Genomic Integration</h4>
              <p>CRISPR-mediated insertion at genomic safe harbor loci ensures single-locus, Mendelian inheritance. Confirmed via Southern blot, RT-PCR, and acetylene reduction assay (ARA).</p>
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="dna-step">
            <div class="step-num">4</div>
            <div class="step-text">
              <h4>Heritable, Seed-Transmissible Trait</h4>
              <p>The trait is inherited uniformly across generations — compatible with existing breeding, seed certification, and commercial licensing pipelines.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div>
        <ul class="solution-points">
          <li><span class="check">&#10003;</span><strong>No external microbial inoculants</strong> — fixation is encoded in the plant genome, not dependent on soil microbiomes or application timing.</li>
          <li><span class="check">&#10003;</span><strong>No annual re-application</strong> — buy the seed once; the trait is inherited by every subsequent generation.</li>
          <li><span class="check">&#10003;</span><strong>Not soil-condition dependent</strong> — works in conventional, no-till, and precision agriculture systems without modification.</li>
          <li><span class="check">&#10003;</span><strong>20–40% fertilizer reduction</strong> — targeted reduction in exogenous nitrogen requirement without yield penalty, validated in controlled trials.</li>
          <li><span class="check">&#10003;</span><strong>Drop-in for existing supply chains</strong> — trait stacks seamlessly with Tier 1 seed company breeding programs and commercial licensing pipelines.</li>
          <li><span class="check">&#10003;</span><strong>Validated in model system</strong> — stable integration and transcriptional activity demonstrated in <em>N. benthamiana</em>; maize (B73) transformation underway.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
